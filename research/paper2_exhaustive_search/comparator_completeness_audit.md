## Candidate-family comparator obligations

- Product mix: exact `3^T`/`4^T` schedules at the small scale, product base-stock, network flow/MILP, stochastic MPC, exact belief DP.
- Reservation/multimodal transport: all booking/mode calendars, max-pressure, threshold/hysteresis, robust reservation, belief DP and stochastic MPC.
- Alternate-route recourse: all full-horizon HOLD/R1/R2 calendars, route thresholds/hysteresis, routing MPC, finite-horizon DP, belief policy, robust doctrine and convex mixtures under identical departures and vehicle-hours.
- Censored demand: all order/report calendars, Bayesian DP, belief-base-stock, optimized constant/`(s,S)` and MPC.
- Lateral transshipment: joint central+lateral schedules, prepositioning, pooling/hold-back doctrines, exact two-node DP and MPC.
- Inspection: all sampling calendars, fixed sampling plans, Bayesian quality thresholds, DP/MPC.
- Finite storage: all space calendars, aggregate buffer frontier, flow/MPC and DP.
- Mission loadout: every fixed pack split, equal/robust doctrine, mission-class thresholds, exact finite-state/belief DP and sealed-demand full-horizon schedules.

If an exact DP is tractable, it is part of the strongest comparator. A learner cannot legitimately beat it except through sampling error; the reduced contract could establish adaptive value but not neural incremental value.
