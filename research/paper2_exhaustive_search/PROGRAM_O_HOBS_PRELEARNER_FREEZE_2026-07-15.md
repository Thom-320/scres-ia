# Program O observable-headroom pre-learner freeze

Status: **contract frozen; no 742-series tape may be opened until a tracked
fit-only execution freeze binds an immutable commit and whole-session watcher.**

Program O has established a custody-verified full-DES perfect-information gap
inside the disclosed two-product researcher extension. It has not established
observable value, learned value, Paper 2, or Paper 3. This gate asks only
whether a deterministic, non-anticipative policy can convert part of that gap.

The primary policy observes only product labels of requests timestamped
strictly before each weekly decision. It uses the exact two-state HMM filter
specified in `contracts/program_o_hobs_prelearner_v1.json`; it never receives
the latent regime, current-week requests, future tape data, the seed, oracle
indices, or score-time metrics. The deliberately narrow observation set makes
future leakage and WIP double-counting easier to falsify.

The denominator remains all 65,536 eight-week open-loop calendars. The best
calendar is re-selected inside every validation bootstrap, so a K3-style
truncated static frontier cannot manufacture H_obs. Delayed, cross-tape
shuffled, wrong-product and no-history policies are mandatory rollout
placebos. All action sequences and observation hashes are retained, and modal
plus phase-only replacements are evaluated under identical resources.

The primary endpoint remains `ret_excel_request_snapshot_v2`. Quantity-weighted
visible ReT is now a preregistered simultaneous guardrail, along with full and
quantity-full ReT, losses, omissions, backlog, worst-product fill, tail risk,
and conservation. The request-snapshot formula is workbook-aligned; its
endogenous same-time convention is a frozen researcher reconstruction and
limits the claim accordingly.

Resource equality is defined by the already-frozen fixed-clock transport
contract: every policy reserves and is charged 112 daily slots and 5,376
downstream vehicle-hours, whether a slot is loaded or empty. Loaded departures,
payload and realized vehicle-hours are still reported. If reviewers adjudicate
loaded trips or payload as additional purchased resources, the gate returns
`STOP_RESOURCE_OR_GUARDRAIL_CONFOUND`; those quantities may not be relabeled
after results.

The old H_obs reservations are retired because the full-DES H_PI retry consumed
part of them. Fresh fit tapes are `7420001–7420048`; fresh pre-learner
validation tapes are `7420049–7420096`. Neither block is learner-virgin. Both
remain sealed at this freeze.

No PPO, recurrent learner, Paper 2 confirmation, or Paper 3 work is authorized
by this document.
