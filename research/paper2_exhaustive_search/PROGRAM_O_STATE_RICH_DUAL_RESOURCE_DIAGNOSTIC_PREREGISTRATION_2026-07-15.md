# Program O — state-rich dual-resource diagnostic preregistration

**Status:** frozen post-hoc diagnostic before execution.

## Purpose

The stopped state-rich fit never executed its information placebos because every
controller failed the earlier actual-transport gate. This new run answers the
unresolved diagnostic question without reopening or relabeling that run.

It uses only burned tapes `7420001–7420048`. Tapes `7420049–7420096` remain sealed.
No tapes, controllers, thresholds, metrics, or parameter cells may be added after
the result is observed.

## Frozen family and denominator

All ten already-frozen classical controllers are evaluated in all four cells. Their
real-observation calendars and outcomes come from the custody-verified stopped fit.
Every placebo calendar is scored against the same complete 65,536-calendar matrices.
There is no neural policy and no new tuning.

## Factorial information audit

The original four placebos perturb total information:

1. state and belief delayed by two weeks;
2. operational state masked and belief neutralized;
3. product channels and belief swapped;
4. complete observation donated by tape `i+17`.

Those controls cannot isolate operational state from regime belief. Therefore this
diagnostic additionally freezes:

- **Operational state given current belief:** retain current belief while masking,
  delaying, or product-swapping inventory, pipeline, backlog, age, and in-flight state.
- **Belief given current operational state:** retain current operational state while
  neutralizing or delaying belief and predicted product share.

For every controller-cell-placebo contrast, inference is paired on the 48 burned tapes
with 10,000 deterministic bootstrap resamples. A placebo family passes only if every
one-sided LCB95 is strictly positive.

## Two resource estimands, reported separately

### Fixed-clock reserved fleet

Resources are the reserved envelope: gross production slots and quantity, charged
dispatch slots, and 5,376 charged downstream vehicle-hours. Realized loading within
that envelope is utilization. Equality of every reserved quantity is binding.

### Pay-per-use transport

Realized loaded departures, payload, and downstream vehicle-hours are resources. The
original strict actual-use and globally matched-throughput frontiers remain binding.

Neither estimand may overwrite the other. A positive fixed-clock diagnostic and a
negative pay-per-use diagnostic must be reported as such.

## Gates and interpretation

For either estimand, a controller must be fixed across a connected component of at
least three cells spanning both persistence and dominant-share levels. Each cell must
have ΔReT at least 0.015, at least 34/48 favorable tapes, clean metric guardrails,
equal reserved resources, genuine action variation, the frozen state-counterfactual
certificate, and all total-information placebos positive. Pay-per-use additionally
requires both actual-use gates.

Incremental operational-state value is a separate estimand and additionally requires
all three operational-state-given-belief placebos. It is not inferred from a Boolean
default or an unexecuted test.

This is a post-hoc development diagnostic. No outcome confirms H_obs, opens sealed
validation, authorizes a learner, confirms Paper 2, or authorizes Paper 3. A signal may
only motivate a new prospectively frozen validation protocol and a domain decision on
the freight accounting model.
