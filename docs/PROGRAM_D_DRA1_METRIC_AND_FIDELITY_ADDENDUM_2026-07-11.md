# DRA-1 preregistration addendum — metric and fidelity corrections

Frozen before DRA-1 implementation or results. This addendum corrects provenance
without altering the risk/action/gate thresholds in the original preregistration.

1. Primary metric is `ret_excel_visible_v1`, not the historical full-ledger
   `mean_ret_excel_formula`. Required companions are clipped ReT, service-loss,
   lost orders, backlog, worst-CSSU service, and mass conservation.
2. Figure 6.2 explicitly documents **two** CSSUs at Op11. The A/B split therefore
   has a thesis basis, but per-CSSU demand, capacity, and local-risk assignment are
   still an explicit structural extension requiring face validation.
3. R22/R23 per-order columns prove that these risks can activate Garrido's ReT
   branch. They do not by themselves prove the proposed spatial localization;
   localization remains the preregistered treatment/placebo manipulation.
4. Remove the statement that Garrido baseline workbook mean ReT is approximately
   one. Raw Cf1 mean is approximately 0.00609 under the workbook formula; values
   vary strongly by risk configuration. All claims remain paired within the same
   tape/regime and never compare absolute levels across contracts.
