# Program P: useful residue, not an authorized program

The proposals reviewed contain a scientifically coherent prospective mechanism:
nonfungible products, mission deadlines, observable criticality, shared batch
capacity, and advance information. They do not yet justify implementation.

## Domain facts required before a contract exists

Garrido must establish, with operational units and timing:

1. at least two materially nonfungible ration classes sharing Op5-Op7 capacity;
2. real request deadlines or mission expiry, distinct from the cap-60 queue rule;
3. decision authority over priority/admission and observable criticality;
4. an advance mission/climate signal available before batch commitment;
5. the physical batch cadence and any setup or minimum-run constraint.

Without these facts, adding deadlines or slack would manufacture the mechanism.

## Diagnostic that is allowed now

Burned Program O tapes may be used descriptively to identify:

- which product drives lower-tail episodes;
- product inventory, WIP, backlog quantity and backlog age;
- the first action divergence from the frozen open-loop comparator;
- lost/unresolved demand and resource equality;
- whether tail episodes reflect product imbalance or general collapse.

The current model has no mission deadlines. Therefore it is prohibited to
compute invented slack, earliest-deadline-first, least-slack-first, or a claim
that a deadline mechanism explains the observed tail. The diagnostic can only
decide whether product imbalance is a plausible question to take to Garrido.
