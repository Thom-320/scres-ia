# Program I — exact replay branching preregistration

The completed Morris screen selected production posture, Op9 dispatch cadence,
and Op10/Op12 transport cadence as the only material implemented decision
families. This gate asks whether those controls reverse their ranking across
physical states and create local perfect-information headroom.

Sixty fresh tapes produce four prefix-balanced states each. Every branch is
reconstructed from the seed, receives the same prefix, and asserts exact raw
observation and flow-ledger identity before applying an alternative. Actions
are evaluated for four and eight weeks. The 4/8-week winning-action agreement
is a binding horizon diagnostic.

Production and Op9 dispatch consume different resources across their actions;
they can establish liveness and diversity but cannot promote in v1. The
transport portfolio preserves the combined nominal departure-opportunity rate:
16/48, 24/24, and 48/16 hours. It is the only resource-equal promotable family.

The output is perfect-information branching only. No tree, observable policy,
confirmation tapes, or learner may be opened by this runner.
